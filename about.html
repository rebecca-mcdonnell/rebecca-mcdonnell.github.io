Hi, I’m Rebecca

I moved to St. Catharines this fall and I’m genuinely excited to be in the Niagara region — the water, the green spaces, and that “new chapter” feeling you get when a place starts to feel like home.

I’m naturally curious and a little obsessed with small details, which is probably why rock collecting stuck so quickly. It started as something calming and fun… and then I blinked and suddenly I had a whole collection (and a camera roll full of “look at this one!!”).

This site is where I share my finds, creative projects, and little updates as I explore Niagara. Some stones are available for purchase, others are just here to be appreciated — either way, every piece has a story behind it.

You can also follow along on Instagram: @rock.girl.cat.mom
